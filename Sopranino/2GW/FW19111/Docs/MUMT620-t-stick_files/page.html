"Inside Out" is an intermedia performance-installation for the Montréal en Lumière/Nuit Blanche/Montréal New Music Festival @Goethe Institut Montréal, 28.02.2015, 8pm-2am.

All media content is created in realtime from spectator data. 2D and 3D video rendering on multiple projection screens, computer vision, stt/tts, topological spatialization, descriptor-based audio synthesis.
